Developers Manual
=================

Introduction
------------

Writing addons for Blender might be confusing for beginners and writing larger applications
such as the RobotDesigner is challenging. Therefore, an infrastructure for this addon has
been written to render debugging, logging and the creation of new functionality as simple
as possible
